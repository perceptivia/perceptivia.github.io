We are here to help you maximize your productivity

In today’s interdisciplinary scientific environment, generating high-impact output is not trivial. Our expert team of analysts, mathematicians, physicists, and statisticians will work collaboratively with your team to take your data analysis to the next level. We provide recommendations, implementation, and training for the application of novel techniques to analyze and model your data.

Analysis Recommendation

Our unique strength is our interdisciplinary background and strong insights into proper scales of analysis in variable contexts.

We’re experts in complex data analysis and are experienced at recognizing patterns in data and determining proper analytical tools.

Implementation

Our experts bring decades of experience and a track record of implementing advanced analysis in scientific research.

We work hand-in-hand with you, as a unified team, to ensure proper and on-time delivery of amazing results.

Training

Our staff is happy to share their expertise with our partners from diverse backgrounds.

We work hard to guide our collaborators through the steps we take to analyze data and to implement our techniques as well as to make meanings of the outcomes of our complex analyses.

Data Visualization

The importance of data visualization in scientific publishing cannot be underestimated. Historically, it has been proven that the more visually appealing papers generate higher impacts.

Our team constantly seeks new tools and techniques for data visualization to enable communicating your results with wider audiences.

Professional Presentation

Powerpoint presentation is one of the most effective ways to communicate science. Mastering the art of presentation, however, can be a long and enduring journey.

We offer our partner teams an effective and unique presentation style and use exercises to apply these techniques to present our collaborative work and ideas more efficiently.

Scientific Writing

Writing high-quality complex scientific publications can be time-consuming and painstaking. You want to speed up the time it takes to submit papers and apply for grants.

Our team of science writers and editors who are all Ph.Ds. and have several years of experience writing highly technical papers are available to assist you in this process.

Activity Scopes

Fast-paced and adaptive development of and/or use of data analyses during the data collection phase of your prospective studies.

Development of and/or use of novel or complex statistical methods.

Developing algorithms to identify units of analysis and define analysis variables.

Active involvement to facilitate writing up and presentation of complex analytical results.
