We seek individuals with expertise in the areas of mathematics, statistics, quantitative and computational sciences. Perceptivia is an equal opportunity employer that values diversity. All employment is decided on the basis of qualifications, merit and business need.

Quantitative Scientist (Ref. #: 2201)

The ideal candidate will have a strong background in a quantitative field, including mathematics, physics, statistics, computer science, or other related fields, and have strong computational background including experience modeling and analyzing various kind of experimental data.

Qualifications

Ability to work closely with teams from diverse scientific fields to identify best analytical methods and to lead strategic initiatives

Graduate degree (preferably Ph.D.) in Mathematics, Physics, Statistics, Computer Science or similar related field

Extensive experience with R or Matlab is a must, knowledge and expertise in Python, or other programming languages is highly desired

Experience communicating analysis and results to various audiences

Experience with designing experiments and data collection is preferred but not required

Responsibilities

Conduct complex exploratory data analysis from diverse data sources to recognize patterns

Generate hypotheses and analyze data to test and interpret results

Communicate findings from advanced data analysis broadly to team and leaders with diverse backgrounds
